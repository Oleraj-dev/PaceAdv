Background

Amrit Ocean Resort & Residences, a first-of-its-kind wellness resort and condominium, engaged us to elevate – The Beach is Just the Beginning – residential campaign for their Resort launch.

Challenge

As a unique wellness destination combining a resort and residences, Amrit posed a challenge for Pace to differentiate it from typical wellness resorts or beachside properties. We needed to convey the developer's wellness-focused vision while positioning it apart from competitors. Additionally, Amrit, being an independent resort, lacked the global audience reach of major flagship brands.

The Solution

Pace embraced the wellness-forward approach with two campaigns to educate guests on Amrit’s unique offerings. The "Well. Beyond..." campaign conveyed Amrit's philosophy: your wellness journey begins with a stay but extends into everyday life. It highlighted the Personal Wellness Support System (PWSS), a personalized plan based on Amrit's five pillars, designed to guide guests long after their visit.

The second campaign, "Expand Your Vocabulary," defined Amrit’s unique philosophies and language, further setting the brand apart. Both campaigns launched using high-end stock photography to market the resort before it was photo-ready.
